# Privacy Policy for Expenses Tracker

**Last updated:** February 15, 2026

## Introduction

Expenses Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address:** Used for account authentication and OTP delivery
- **Password:** Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt)

### Financial Information
- **Expense Records:** Amount, category, description, and date of expenses you create
- **Budget Information:** Your monthly budget goals and tracking data

### Technical Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Log Data:** App usage patterns, error logs for debugging purposes

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **Authentication & Security**
   - Verifying your identity through email OTP
   - Securing your account with encrypted passwords
   - Maintaining session security with JWT tokens

2. **App Functionality**
   - Storing and displaying your expense records
   - Tracking your budget goals
   - Providing personalized expense insights

3. **Service Improvement**
   - Analyzing app usage to improve features
   - Debugging and fixing technical issues
   - Enhancing user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (industry-standard)
- Database connections are encrypted
- JWT tokens for secure session management
- Regular security audits and updates

### Data Location
- Backend hosted on Render (cloud infrastructure)
- Database hosted on MongoDB Atlas (encrypted at rest)
- Email service provided by Resend

## Data Sharing

**We DO NOT:**
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your financial data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing without consent

**We MAY share data with:**
- **Service Providers:** Render (hosting), MongoDB (database), Resend (email) - only as necessary for app functionality
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data:** View all data we have about you
2. **Delete Your Account:** Request complete account and data deletion
3. **Export Your Data:** Request a copy of your data
4. **Correct Your Data:** Update or correct your information
5. **Withdraw Consent:** Stop using the app at any time
